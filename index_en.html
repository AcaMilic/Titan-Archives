TITAN ARCHIVES APROACH

Documentary material created by legal entities on the territory of Serbia is treated as a cultural good, until archival material is classified and separated from it. It is a well-known practice that legal entities do not implement the Decree on office and archive operations in a quality manner, and that leads to destruction, loss of documentation that is important for property and legal affairs, financial transactions and realization of workers' rights.

In order to be able to fully understand your business as well as the business of entities with documentary material, in the first interview we need to meet with the persons in charge of the archive and office, as well as to inspect the archive depot and basic records of office operations (business protocol). After the interview and inspection of the depot and insight into the business of the office, we will make a proposal for the implementation of the software solution and propose measures to improve the business.

We are of the opinion that our experience in arranging archival material and documentary material, drafting normative acts (Rulebook on office and archival operations and List of categories of documentary material with retention periods) by establishing basic records of archival business (Archival book) and implementing software solutions covering complete office and archival business, significantly improved your business, business of entities, maximized the use of existing resources as well as enabled significant savings in financial terms.
